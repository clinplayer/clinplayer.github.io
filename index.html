Cheng Lin 林铖

Ph.D., Researcher

chlin@connect.hku.hk

I am currently a researcher at miHoYo. Previously I was a senior researcher at Tencent Games .
I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from The Univeristy of Hong Kong (HKU), advised by Prof. Wenping Wang. I visited Visual Computing Group at Technical University of Munich (TUM), advised by Prof. Matthias Nießner. Before that, I completed my B.Eng. degree at Shandong University working on geometry and graphics. My research interests include geometric modeling, 3D vision, shape analysis and computer graphics.
